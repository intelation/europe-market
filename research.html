Potential Revenue Streams (hypotheses)

Based on Intelation's capabilities and preseed status, these are potential revenue models for the European market:

  • SaaS subscriptions for the privacy platform (tiered by features/scale).
  • Usage-based pricing for privacy processing (credits tied to API calls, records, or data volume).
  • Enterprise licensing for advanced privacy-preserving computation with support and SLAs.
  • Advisory retainers and fixed-fee projects (GDPR readiness, cross-border compliance, gap analysis, market entry compliance).
  • Paid onboarding and implementation packages (integration, deployment hardening, workflow embedding).
  • Training and enablement (workshops, role-based training, certifications).
  • Partner/channel revenue (referrals and delivery partnerships with consultancies, law firms, SIs).
