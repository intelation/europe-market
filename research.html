United Kingdom

Country: United Kingdom

Visa options (Indian staff):
- Main visa routes: 
  1. Skilled Worker Visa: For sponsored employees with a job offer in an eligible occupation. Requires the UK company to be a Home Office-licensed sponsor. Path to settlement after 5 years.
  2. Innovator Founder Visa: For entrepreneurs with an innovative, viable, and scalable business idea endorsed by an approved body. Path to settlement after 3 years.
  3. UK Expansion Worker Visa: For senior managers or specialist employees of an overseas company to establish a UK branch. No path to settlement; maximum stay is 2 years.
- Sponsor requirements: For the Skilled Worker and Expansion Worker visas, Intelation's UK entity would need to obtain a sponsor license from the Home Office. This involves an application process and compliance obligations.
- Typical timeline: Visa processing is typically 3 weeks from outside the UK. Sponsor license applications can take several months.
- Renewability: Skilled Worker visas are extendable. The Innovator Founder visa can be extended. The Expansion Worker visa is limited to a 2-year total stay.
