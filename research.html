Objective

Assess the feasibility and best path for establishing Intelation in the European market (EU + UK), including identifying the highest-potential customer segments, priority countries, regulatory and competitive considerations, and the go-to-market approach required to enter and scale.

Country Selection Framework

Criteria (equal weight)

  1. Business establishment: setup/operating friction, cost base, hiring practicality.
  2. Visa options: realistic routes for Indian staff (founders and early hires), timelines, renewability.
  3. Funding access: depth of preseed/seed ecosystem fit for privacy/compliance/enterprise SaaS.
Scoring: each criterion is scored 0–5 per country.

Formula

Total Score = 0.33 × B + 0.33 × V + 0.33 × F
B = Business establishment score, V = Visa options score, F = Funding access score.

Outputs

  • Ranked list across a candidate set.
  • Top 5–8 countries selected for deep-dive research.
  • Watchlist countries for lighter tracking.

Potential Revenue Streams (hypotheses)

  • SaaS subscriptions for the privacy platform (tiered by features/scale).
  • Usage-based pricing for privacy processing (credits tied to API calls, records, or data volume).
  • Enterprise licensing for advanced privacy-preserving computation with support and SLAs.
  • Advisory retainers and fixed-fee projects (GDPR readiness, cross-border compliance, gap analysis, market entry compliance).
  • Paid onboarding and implementation packages (integration, deployment hardening, workflow embedding).
  • Training and enablement (workshops, role-based training, certifications).
  • Partner/channel revenue (referrals and delivery partnerships with consultancies, law firms, SIs).
